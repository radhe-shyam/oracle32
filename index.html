  • Apna Ghar Ashram Jodhpur Camp Dec 2016

    Oracle 32 Jodhpur centre organised a free dental camp at 'Apna Ghar' Ashram on 17/12/2016. More than 150 inmates participated which included the homeless elderlies as well as the mentally challenged. It was nothing short of challenge but #o32jodhpur coordinated the event smoothly with skill, discipline and planning. Special cases needing immediate attention were later treated free of cost by Oracle 32.

  • Oracle32 Treatment camp : Jodhpur centre

    As promised, we rendered free treatment at our Jodhpur centre to the inmates of Apna Ghar Ashram Jodhpur who were prescribed immediate dental intervention at the diagnostic camp 3 days back in ashram...

  • Satchel Jammu Art and Dental camp

    In continuation with the 'Nanhi Muskaan' initiative, Oracle 32 Jammu in collaboration with Satchel - the matamal organised a first of it's kind 'Art and Dental camp' at Satchel on 10-12-2016.The Oracle 32 team comprising of Dr. Vibhuti Kaul, Mr. Sahil Dogra and Mr. Gagan Pandey screened more than 100 children who participated in this event. Selected children were even treated with 'atraumatic restorative technique' at the venue. An interactive lecture on Oral Health and hygiene maintenance was delivered by Dr. Rudra Kaul. He even demonstrated the chief brushing technique to the little ones. Free Colgate toothpastes and toothbrushes were distributed among all the participants.
